    There's Lead in That?!

    Lead remains in many products. And the CDC says no level of lead is safe for kids.

    Quiz

    Which is not a symptom of lead poisoning?
    You are correct!
    Incorrect

    The correct answer is: Testicular

    Hearing loss is not a symptom of lead poisoning. Symptoms may include high blood pressure, headaches, loss of memory or concentration, among other conditions.
